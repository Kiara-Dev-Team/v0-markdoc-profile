# About Dave Ishii

I'm a Fintech and AI entrepreneur with a background in pure mathematics, fashion business, and international market development, based in Tokyo, Japan.

## Background

As Founder & CEO of Kiara Inc., I build products that bridge technology and human experience. My work spans:

- **Human-Finance Interaction**: Researching and developing innovative fintech solutions that put humans first
- **Digital Identity (eKYC)**: Pioneering secure and efficient identity verification systems
- **International Business**: Leading cross-cultural partnerships and market expansion across Europe and Asia
- **AI Innovation**: Creating AI-powered tools that break down language barriers and enhance communication

## Career Journey

My career has taken me from the fashion industry at ITOCHU Corporation, where I worked as an expatriate in the UK and Italy developing global brands, to founding Kiara Inc. and revolutionizing multilingual team communication.
